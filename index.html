Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 250g Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can Tomato Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Cook spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and let simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve the sauce over spaghetti. Enjoy!

Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts, diced
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can Coconut Milk
  • 2 tbsp Curry Powder
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Heat olive oil in a pot and sauté onions and garlic until soft.
  2. Add diced chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in curry powder and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve with rice or naan. Enjoy!
